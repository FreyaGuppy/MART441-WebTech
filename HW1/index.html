A memory of playing a game

I live near the Missoula Public Library. At the library, they have a decent collection of board games that you can check out, so, frequently, when I have friends over, we will pop over to the library to see if there are any games that look interesting. Recently, I checked out the card game, Gubs. Gubs is pretty simple, "a game of wit and luck," as it states on the tin. But the worldbuilding is very cute and silly. My roommate and I grew quite fond of it after a couple of playthroughs. She often has a hard time grasping or enjoying more complex games, they're just not her thing. But Gubs was one we could pull out and play together when others came over. Simple, quick, silly, it has become a favorite for casual play with a special place in my heart.
